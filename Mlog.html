- Are clients able to specify what time the cleaner should arrive?

You can schedule the cleaner's arrival at your location as convenient for you.


- Mode of payment?

Once the job is completed, the cash will be collected. Additionally, the customer can pay the cleaner directly using the available online transcation.


- Can I reschedule a cleaning service?

Keeping in mind that life can be unpredictable, we work with your schedule to provide cleaning at a time that is convenient for you. When cancelling or rescheduling a cleaning service, please inform us as soon as possible so we can try to accommodate your needs.


- Do I need to prepare before the cleaners show up?

If you are able, please pick up any items (toys, clothing, papers) before we arrive. Make sure your pets are in an area where they will be comfortable while we clean.
